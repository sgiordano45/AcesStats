How the Ring-Chain Works (11 teams)

Each week, 4 teams are bridge teams — they play once as a chain endpoint on two different nights (2 games total across 2 nights). The other 7 teams are DH teams — they take the middle position in one chain and play both games on the same night. Every team plays exactly 2 games every week (1 home + 1 away). Roles rotate each week for balance.

Nights 1–3 are full 4-team chains (3 games). Night 4 is a short 3-team chain (2 games) that closes the ring — it has one short-DH team and reuses two bridge teams as its endpoints.
