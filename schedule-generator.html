With 11 teams, every week one team must either sit out or play an extra game. Choose how to handle this:

🎉 Opening Day — 6 Games, All Teams Play

Opening Day is generated specially with 6 games starting at 3:45 PM ET. All 11 teams play a single game (3:45–7:45 PM), then the DH team plays one additional game at 8:45 PM. You'll select the DH team in Step 3.

Opening Day is always Sunday. Select which additional weekdays are available for DHs:

☀️ 10-Team Summer Schedule:
  • Opening Day (Sunday): 5 single games starting at 3:45 PM ET — all teams play once
  • Week 1 Mon–Thu: 4 DHs (8 teams get a DH, 2 teams rest)
  • Week 2+: Sun–Thu — each team plays 1 DH per week (5 DHs/night)
  • DH Time Slots: 7:45 PM and 8:45 PM
🍂 Fall League Schedule:
  • 12 games per team
  • Primary Day: Sundays
  • Time Slots: 8:00 AM, 9:00 AM, 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM, 12:00 PM (5 games per Sunday)
  • Frequency: Each team plays once per Sunday
  • Optional weeknight games for late season/makeup games
  • Minimum 7 players to start
