Add Your Schedule to Your Calendar

Sync your team's games or the full league schedule to Google, Apple, or Outlook — with automatic updates if anything changes.

📍 Where to Find the Calendar Button
📋 The 🗓️ Add to Calendar button appears in two places: the Season page (full league schedule) and your Team page (your team's games only). Both work the same way.
1
Choose your starting point

For your team's games only: go to your Team page via the Teams nav link. For all league games: go to the Season page via the Season nav link.

2
Find the "🗓️ Add to Calendar" button

On the Team page it's in the Season Schedule & Results section header. On the Season page it's near the top of the schedule. Click it to open the export dropdown.

3
Pick your method

Choose from Subscribe, Download .ics, Add to Google Calendar, or Copy URL — each described below.

📲 Your Export Options
🔗
Subscribe (Auto-Updates) — Recommended
Click Subscribe (auto-updates) to open a popup with a live calendar URL. Add it to your app and the schedule will stay current automatically — any reschedules or changes sync within about an hour.

Step-by-step instructions for each app are shown directly in that popup, and also listed in the section below.
⬇️
Download .ics File
Click Download .ics file to save a one-time snapshot of the schedule.
  1. Open the downloaded file
  2. Your device will ask which calendar to add events to
  3. Choose your calendar and confirm
Note: This won't update if the schedule changes later.
📆
Add to Google Calendar
Click Add to Google Calendar — a new tab opens in Google Calendar with the subscription pre-filled.
  1. Click "Add calendar" to confirm
  2. All games appear under "Other calendars"
  3. Auto-updates just like Subscribe
📋
Copy URL
Click Copy URL to copy the calendar link straight to your clipboard. Use this to manually paste into any calendar app that supports subscribed calendars.
💡 Best pick: Use Subscribe or Add to Google Calendar — both stay live and will reflect any rainouts or reschedules automatically.
📱 Subscribe Instructions by App

After clicking Subscribe, a popup shows the URL with these same instructions. Here's the quick reference:

📱
iPhone / iOS Calendar
  1. Go to Settings → Calendar → Accounts
  2. Tap Add Account → Other
  3. Tap Add Subscribed Calendar
  4. Paste the URL and tap Next
  5. Tap Save
📆
Google Calendar (Web)
  1. Open Google Calendar on desktop
  2. Click the + next to "Other calendars"
  3. Select From URL
  4. Paste the URL and click Add calendar
📧
Outlook
  1. Go to Calendar view
  2. Click Add calendar → From Internet
  3. Paste the URL
  4. Click OK
🍎
Mac Calendar
  1. Open the Calendar app
  2. Go to File → New Calendar Subscription
  3. Paste the URL
  4. Click Subscribe
❓ Common Questions
Q
Will my calendar update if a game gets rescheduled?

Yes — if you used Subscribe, Add to Google Calendar, or Copy URL, your calendar syncs automatically within about an hour. A downloaded .ics file won't update.

Q
It shows all teams' games — can I filter to just mine?

Yes — use the button on your Team page instead of the Season page. It automatically exports only your team's games. Navigate to Teams → your team, then use the 🗓️ Add to Calendar button in the schedule section.

Q
The button isn't showing up — what do I do?

Make sure you're on the Season page (not a team or standings page). Try refreshing if it doesn't appear right away.

Q
What info is included in each event?

Each game includes the date, time, field location, and the two teams playing.
